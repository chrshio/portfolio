The new Small Bag is designed to last.

Made out of a tough 14 oz Canvas.

Handy interior pocket and loops to customise your Small Bag with our Net Pocket, fully customisable to clip in and out. Not great for pencils, pocket change or diamonds but handy for life's other essentials you need to stash. Available to colour block your look in blue, orange or green.

The Lavender colour has been garment dyed giving it a unique two tone shade with contrast stitching. It is 10% smaller due to the dye process and has a soft vintage look and feel.

17oz cotton canvas
Interior pocket

Ethically manufactured

Garment dyed in small batches so no two will be the same.

There will be small imperfections and irregularities which are to be celebrated!
